WURFL Database

Capabilities that a developer, with access to WURFL, can use and test against the device that is requesting the application

Capability Description
has_cellular_radio Device has cellular technology (Most probably a phone but not necessarily) Perhaps a data-only device such as kindle.
can_assign_phone_number True if device is a mobile phone and may have a phone number associated to it.
is_wireless_device True if a device is Wireless (WiFi, 3G, LTE, etc.) especifically a mobile phone or a tablet are considered as wireless mobile, but a desktop PC or a laptop are not.
